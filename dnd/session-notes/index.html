Alrighty, let's review Session 1 of the Switcheroo Campaign! We opened with in the lowly town of Saltine Grove. In fact, we open in it's magicless prison, where our 4 yet to be party members; Alros, Arzu (in cell 1), Sora and Faeryn (in cell 2), meet eachother for the first time. They also encounter two guards, a young human who's new to the job named Clifton "Clif" Laungsteel and a older gruff half-orc named Brom Duststone. All four get led down by the guards one by one to the courtroom for their trials, overseen and judged by the dramatic but stubborn Justice Justin. He's a spritly early adult tiefling who throughly enjoys a show, but unfortunately for our intrepid adventurers, he has a 100% conviction rate, and all of them end up back in their cells, one awaiting a retrial. One event that occurs during the start of the third trial, the trial of Arzu Amaskiir Vs the Court of Saltine Grove, The Justice is late arriving, and a heated argument is heard between him and an unheard voice from their Quarters, before a crash is heard and Brom, the guard, is called to the back and he returns with a small threaded sack with something inside, he quickly exits the courtroom and returns after during Arzu's breakdown after His Honour's pressure. Other notable mentions include -Alros speaks to their God, Judgement to help them through the trial -Sora accuses Faeryn of being insane during her trial -Because of Sora's behaviour, she's charged with Contempt of Court and awaits a retrial -Faeryn injures themself badly in their cell to try convince the courts Sora is the insane one -Faeryn almost drops unconscious We left off our session with everyone in their cells except for Faeryn, who's still injured and currently getting tended to by Brom and Clifton. Sora still awaiting a retrial, Arzu still having a panic attack and everyone except the guards awaiting sentencing
